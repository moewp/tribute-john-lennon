John Lennon

The English Singer, Songwriter, Musician and Peace Activist

Here's a time line of John Lennon's life:

  • 1940 - He was born in Liverpool England
  • 1956 - Became involved in the skiffle craze as a tennager
  • 1960 - He formed the Quarrymen, which evolved into the Beatles
  • 1961 - The Beatles, with John Lennon as front man, debut at Liverpool's Cavern Club
  • 1962 - John marries Cynthia Powell
  • 1964 - The Beatles begin their first American tour, playing on The Ed Sullivan Show and at The Coliseum in Washington, D.C. and Carnegie Hall in New York City
  • 1965 - The Beatles play to a crowd of over 60,000 fans at Shea Stadium in New York City
  • 1966 - John states that the Beatles are more popular than Jesus, setting off a backlash against the group and spurring the public burning of Beatle albums
  • 1968 - John and Cynthia are divorced.
  • 1969 - John and Yoko are married on Gibraltar.
  • 1971 - John records "Imagine" at his home studio at Tittenhurst Park
  • 1972 - Senator Strom Thurmond tells Attorney General John Mitchell that John should be deported because he consorts with known radicals such as Jerry Rubin and Abbie Hoffman
  • 1973 - John leaves for Los Angeles, beginning his eighteen-month separation from Yoko
  • 1974 - John makes his final concert appearance at Madison Square Garden with Elton John
  • 1975 - John and Yoko are reunited
  • 1976 - John's application to remain in the United States as a permanent resident is approved
  • 1980 - John Lennon is assassinated outside the Dakota at the age of 40
